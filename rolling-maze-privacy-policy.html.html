Privacy Policy for Rolling Maze


What Information We Collect

We only collect game data, such as the level the player has reached, skins the player has purchased with in-game currency, and other similar game-related data. We do not collect any personal information from our users.


How We Collect Information

We collect game data automatically as users play the game. We do not collect any information directly from users.


How We Use Information

We use the game data we collect to improve the game experience for our users. We do not use the information for any other purpose.


How We Share Information

We do not share any of the game data we collect with third parties, except for Unity ads which we use to show advertisements in the game. We do not have control over the data collected by Unity ads. Please refer to Unity's privacy policy for more information.


Accessing, Updating, and Deleting Information

Since we do not collect any personal information from our users, there is no information for users to access, update, or delete.


Security

We do not have any security measures in place to protect the game data we collect.


Minimum Age

Rolling Maze is intended for users aged 13 and above.


Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify users of any changes by posting the updated privacy policy on our website.
